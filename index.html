About Me

Hi, I’m Rana Thakur — an AI Specialist and Data Scientist with over two decades of experience turning complex ideas into practical, high-impact AI solutions.

My career spans designing Generative AI, LLM, and Machine Learning systems that help organizations work smarter, faster, and more securely. I’ve worked on everything from fine-tuning Llama models for secure, in-house AI assistants to building multi-agent RAG systems that deliver context-aware answers in real time.

I believe AI should be responsible, explainable, and useful — not just a buzzword. That’s why I focus on solutions that respect privacy, security, fairness, and ethics, while still pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.

Outside of work, I love sharing knowledge through my blogs and contributing to open-source AI libraries that make advanced tools more accessible to the community. My open-source projects reflect my belief in collaboration — the idea that the best solutions are often built together.

When I’m not coding or writing, you might find me exploring new technologies, experimenting with AI-powered workflows, or mentoring teams to help them get the most out of AI.

If you’re looking for someone who can bridge business needs with cutting-edge AI technology — and deliver solutions that actually work in production — let’s connect.
